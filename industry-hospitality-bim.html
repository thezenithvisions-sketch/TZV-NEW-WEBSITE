BIM Services for Hospitality Projects

Hotels and resorts blend repeated guest rooms with bespoke public spaces and heavy services. We coordinate both, so design intent survives all the way to the guest.

Get a project quote

Why BIM for hospitality projects

Hospitality projects are two buildings in one: highly repeated guest-room floors that reward efficient, reusable modeling, and one-off public spaces, lobbies, restaurants, spas, where bespoke interiors and dense services must be coordinated to the millimetre. Hospitality BIM handles both, keeping the repeated areas efficient and the feature spaces clash-free.

We deliver coordinated Revit models, interior and FF&E documentation, MEP coordination and photoreal visualization for hotels, resorts, restaurants and serviced apartments.

What you get

  • Guest-room types modeled once and rolled out across every floor
  • Feature public spaces coordinated with bespoke joinery and services
  • FF&E and finish schedules generated from the model
  • Photoreal visualization for investors and pre-opening marketing

FAQ

Hospitality BIM, answered

Can you model repeated guest-room types efficiently?

Yes. We build each room type once and deploy it across every floor, so schedules and quantities stay accurate and changes propagate automatically, the most efficient way to model a hotel.

Do you handle FF&E and interior documentation for hospitality?

We produce coordinated interior documentation, FF&E and finish schedules, and bespoke joinery details for lobbies, restaurants and feature spaces.

Can you visualize a hotel before construction?

Yes, we create photoreal renders and walkthroughs for investor decks and pre-opening marketing, built from the coordinated model.

Planning a hospitality project?

Tell us about it. We deliver accurate, coordinated, build-ready BIM for hospitality projects worldwide, and we respond within one business day.
