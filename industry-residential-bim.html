Why BIM for residential projects

Residential projects look simple and rarely are. Tight margins, fast programs, repeated unit types and demanding clients leave no room for documentation that drifts out of sync. Residential BIM gives you one coordinated model that every plan, schedule and quantity flows from, so a late change to a unit type updates everywhere at once instead of becoming a variation.

We work with developers, architects and home builders to deliver accurate Revit models, permit-ready documentation and clash-free coordination for villas, apartments, townhouses and master-planned communities worldwide.

What you get

  • Repeatable unit types modeled once and reused across the scheme
  • Accurate area and unit schedules generated straight from the model
  • Permit and GFC documentation built to local building codes
  • Architecture, structure and MEP coordinated before site

FAQ

Residential BIM, answered

Do you handle residential permit and GFC documentation?

Yes. We produce permit sets and Good-for-Construction drawings compliant with local building codes across the USA, UK, Canada, Australia and India, fully coordinated with the BIM model.

Can you model repeated unit types efficiently?

Absolutely. We model each unit type once and reuse it across the development, so schedules and quantities stay accurate and any change updates everywhere automatically.

Which residential project types do you cover?

Villas, single-family homes, townhouses, apartment buildings and master-planned communities, at any LOD from concept through construction.
