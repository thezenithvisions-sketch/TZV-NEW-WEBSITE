CAD Drafter

Full-time On-site · Indore Drafting

Produce precise 2D construction and shop drawings in AutoCAD from designs, sketches, scans and PDF markups, keeping our deliverables clean, standards-compliant and ready for site.

What you’ll do

  • Prepare GA drawings, plans, sections, elevations and details.
  • Convert sketches, PDFs and scans into accurate CAD drawings.
  • Maintain title blocks, layering and drawing standards.
  • Incorporate redlines and manage revisions efficiently.

What we’re looking for

  • Diploma or degree in Civil, Architecture or related field.
  • 1–3 years of hands-on AutoCAD experience.
  • Strong grasp of drafting conventions and dimensioning.
  • Eye for detail, accuracy and on-time delivery.

Revit BIM Modeller

Full-time On-site · Indore BIM

Develop coordinated Revit models across architecture, structure and MEP at LOD 100–500, with complete sheet documentation, schedules and clash-aware coordination for AEC clients worldwide.

What you’ll do

  • Build and maintain Revit models from CAD, scan and PDF inputs.
  • Produce floor plans, sections, elevations, schedules and sheets.
  • Coordinate disciplines and help resolve clashes early.
  • Create and manage Revit families and project templates.

What we’re looking for

  • Degree or diploma in Architecture, Civil or related field.
  • 1–4 years of Autodesk Revit experience.
  • Understanding of LOD, BIM standards and documentation.
  • Navisworks / Scan-to-BIM exposure is a plus.
