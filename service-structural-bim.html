Structural BIM Service

Services / Structural BIM Service

Structural models and shop drawings that engineers and fabricators can build straight from.

At The Zenith Visions, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge Structural BIM Services that revolutionize how structural design integrates with advanced technology. Our approach focuses on developing intelligent, data-rich 3D models that optimize the analysis, design, and documentation processes.

Software

Revit, Tekla, AutoCAD

Level of detail

LOD 200 to 400

Typical lead

4 to 12 days

Disciplines

RCC, Steel, Precast

Our take

Built for the fabricator

A structural model is only useful if the people bending steel and pouring concrete can rely on it. We detail to fabrication standards and flag clashes early, so what gets issued is what gets built.

How we work

One coordinated frame

We work from the engineer's design intent and keep the model aligned with architecture and MEP throughout. Quantities, connections and rebar all come from the same model, so nothing drifts out of sync.

What we offer

Sub-services

Structure BIM Modeling

Concrete, steel and precast structural models to your required LOD.

Steel Structure Modeling

Detailed steelwork models ready for connection design and fabrication.

Rebar Detailing & Modeling

Reinforcement modelled and scheduled for accurate bar bending and quantities.

Clash Detection

The structural model checked against architecture and MEP to catch conflicts early.
