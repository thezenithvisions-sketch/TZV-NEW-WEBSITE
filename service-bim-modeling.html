BIM Modeling Services

Services / BIM Modeling Services

Full-lifecycle BIM modeling — from concept through construction-ready deliverables.

The Zenith Visions provides end-to-end BIM modeling services for architects, engineers, contractors and developers worldwide. We build Revit models that are coordinated, data-rich and ready to issue — covering Architecture, Structural, MEP and Scan to BIM disciplines at LOD 100 through LOD 500. Our team of dedicated Revit professionals works to your standards, your templates and your timeline, so you spend less time correcting models and more time doing design work.

Software

Revit, Navisworks, AutoCAD

LOD range

LOD 100 to LOD 500

Disciplines

Architecture, Structure, MEP

Output

RVT, NWC, IFC, DWG, PDF

Our approach

One model, every discipline coordinated

We build a federated model from the start. Architecture, structure and MEP share a common coordinate system and are clash-checked before anything leaves our desk, so you receive a model ready for review, not one that creates work for your team.

Why clients choose us

Your templates, your standards, zero reformatting

We work inside your Revit template, respect your layer naming, view discipline and sheet setup. Models drop straight into your project without reformatting, renaming or re-linking. We adapt to your workflow, not the other way around.

Quality process

Multi-stage QA before every delivery

Every model goes through a formal QA checklist: geometry accuracy, element categorisation, parameter completeness, sheet compliance and Navisworks clash review. Nothing is delivered unless it passes. You get a QA report with each submission so you can track what was checked.

What we deliver

Sub-services

Architecture BIM

Intelligent Revit architecture models from schematic design through construction documents. Floor plans, elevations, sections, schedules and 3D views — all linked, all consistent, all ready to coordinate.

Structural BIM

Concrete and steel structural Revit models with rebar detailing, framing layouts, connection design and bar-bending schedules coordinated against the architectural model.

MEP BIM

Mechanical, electrical, plumbing and fire-protection Revit models built to your specification. Full clash detection against architecture and structure before handover.

Scan to BIM

Point cloud processing and as-built Revit modeling from laser scan data. Accurate existing-conditions models for renovation, retrofit and facility management at LOD 300–400.

Revit Family Creation

Custom parametric loadable and system families — architectural components, MEP equipment, structural connections, furniture — built to your shared parameter standards.

BIM Coordination & Clash Detection

Federated model review and clash resolution using Navisworks or BIM 360. Clash reports, coordination meeting support and resolved model delivery.

Common questions

FAQs

What file types do you need to start a BIM modeling project?

We can start from PDFs, DWG drawings, point cloud data, sketches or an existing Revit file. We will assess what you provide and confirm the LOD we can achieve from that input.

Which Revit version do you work in?

We work in Revit 2022 through 2025 and can save down to older versions where needed. We always confirm the version with you before starting.

What is the typical turnaround for a BIM modeling project?

A single-discipline model for a medium-sized building typically takes 3 to 10 business days. Multi-discipline coordinated models take longer depending on complexity. We provide a firm schedule with every quote.

Do you follow ISO 19650 or BIM Execution Plan requirements?

Yes. We can work to a client-supplied BIM Execution Plan (BEP) and follow ISO 19650 naming and folder conventions. If you do not have a BEP, we use a standard template and share it for your review.

Can you handle large complex projects?

Yes. We have modeled hospitals, airports, high-rise towers and campus developments. We use worksets, linked models and phasing to manage complexity and maintain model performance throughout the project.
