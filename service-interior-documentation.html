Interior Documentation

Services / Interior Documentation

Interior models and documentation sets that take a fit-out from concept to install.

Interior documentation is a critical part of the design and construction process, ensuring accurate translation of design intent into executable plans. At The Zenith Visions, we specialize in delivering comprehensive interior documentation services.

Software

Revit, AutoCAD

Level of detail

LOD 200 to 400

Typical lead

3 to 10 days

Output

RVT, DWG, PDF

Our take

Documentation that installs

A beautiful interior render does not help the joiner on site. We produce the dimensioned, scheduled drawings that turn a concept into something a contractor can price and build.

How we work

From concept to setting-out

We build the interior model, add furniture and fixture families, then generate finish schedules and setting-out drawings from it, all coordinated so the numbers always agree.

What we offer

Sub-services

BIM Interior Modeling

Detailed interior models with furniture, fixtures and finishes.

Interior Design Documentation

Documentation that translates design intent into buildable plans.

Joinery Design & Details

Joinery and millwork detailed for fabrication and install.

Quantity Take-off

Accurate material and quantity take-offs straight from the model.
