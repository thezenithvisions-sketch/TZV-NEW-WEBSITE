About Us

About us

We are The Zenith Visions, a global BIM partner that helps architects, engineers and contractors turn designs into precise, coordinated, construction-ready models, on time and without the rework.

About Our Company

The Zenith Visions is a BIM services partner working at the intersection of design, data and delivery. From your first sketch, scan or CAD file through to a fully coordinated model, every deliverable is shaped by the same question: is this accurate, buildable and ready for site? We work as a seamless extension of AEC teams worldwide.

Our Mission

We make BIM effortless for the teams we serve, delivering precise, coordinated models and documentation that cut design uncertainty, speed up delivery and remove costly rework. Quality is the floor, not the ceiling.

We see BIM as the backbone of how the built world is designed and delivered. Our vision is to be the most dependable BIM partner in the AEC industry, the team firms trust with their most complex models, anywhere in the world.

Our Principles

Core principles that shape every model we deliver

Accuracy

Every model is built to spec and checked against your inputs. Precision is not something we add at the end, it is where we start.

Coordination

Clash-free, discipline-coordinated models are our standard, not an upsell. We resolve conflicts in the model so they never reach your site.

Company History

Our Story

Milestones marking our growth into a global BIM partner trusted across the AEC industry.

2019

Getting Our Start

The Zenith Visions opens with a small team of BIM specialists and one promise: accurate, on-time models that AEC firms can build from.

2020

First Global Clients

Architects and contractors across three countries start outsourcing their architectural BIM and drafting to our growing team.

2021

Scan to BIM & As-Builts

We add high-detail scan-to-BIM and as-built modelling, taking on retrofit and heritage work up to LOD 500.

2022

Full MEP Coordination

Navisworks clash detection and full mechanical, electrical and plumbing coordination become core services, with dedicated discipline teams.

2024

1,000+ Projects Delivered

We pass a thousand completed projects across nine service lines, from Revit families and drafting to full 3D visualization.

2025

A Global BIM Partner

Now serving 200+ clients across 10+ countries, trusted as a true extension of their in-house teams.
