About Us

About us

We are the people behind The Zenith Visions — a studio of architects, planners and craftspeople who believe great spaces are built on equal parts discipline and imagination.

About Our Company

The Zenith Visions is a multidisciplinary architecture practice working at the intersection of climate, craft and community. From the first sketch through to final handover, every drawing on our desk is shaped by the same question: how will this building serve the people inside it and the place around it?

Our Mission

We craft architectural experiences that hold up to daily life — buildings that work harder, age better, and feel like they belong to their context. Sustainability is the floor, not the ceiling.

Rich Wilson CEO, The Zenith Visions

Vision

We see architecture as quiet infrastructure for the lives people want to lead. Our vision is to shape neighborhoods, campuses and homes that read as honest, generous and inevitable a decade from now.

Our Process

From Blank Page to Built Reality

Four phases we move every project through — from listening to handover.

Be a Happy Client
Step 01

Conceptual Design

We open every project by listening. Site walks, neighbourhood research and stakeholder conversations turn the brief into a tested concept — sketches, massing diagrams and the constraints worth defending.

Step 02

Schematic Design

With direction agreed, we draw the building. Plans, sections, massing studies and early 3D views resolve the architecture into something you can walk through before the first wall is built.

Step 03

Design Development

Every line earns its place. Materials, structure, MEP coordination, lighting and acoustic details are resolved alongside energy modelling so the finished building performs as well as it reads.

Step 04

Construction Documentation

We hand the contractor a clear, accurate, build-ready set — architectural, structural and MEP drawings priced and permitted, supported by us on site until the building opens.

Our Principles

Core principles that shape our work and culture

01

Creativity

We treat every brief as an open question. The studio rewards designs that arrive at the obvious answer last — once the surprising one has been properly tested.

02

Sustainability

Energy use, embodied carbon and material life are not features bolted on at the end. They sit alongside structure and light as first-order inputs from day one.

03

Collaboration

Engineers, makers, clients and neighbours each sharpen the work in ways we cannot do alone. We design with the room, not around it.

Company History

Our Story

Milestones marking our growth from a two-person studio to a 50-strong practice.

2007

Getting Our Start

Sarah Taylor and James Thompson sign the lease on a one-room studio with twenty-five combined years between them and a deliberate first project list of three.

2010

Growing to 10 Strong

Five architects and a small operations team join the studio — enough breadth to take on civic work without slowing the residential pipeline.

2014

Award-Winning Hospitality Projects

Three boutique hotels open inside twelve months. The work earns hospitality industry awards and our first international publication.

2017

Residential Portfolios Expanding

Bespoke homes move from a quarter of the book to the majority of it — country estates, townhouses and high-rise apartments tailored around how each family actually lives.

2021

Next-Gen Technologies

VR walkthroughs, printed massing models and parametric structural studies graduate from experiments to everyday tools across every project.

2025

The Future is Green

Fifty people strong. Over four-fifths of live projects meet our internal sustainability brief, and five new commercial buildings open net-zero on energy.
