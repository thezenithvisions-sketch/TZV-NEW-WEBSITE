UK Engineering Firm: Five Projects Delivered On Time with Zero MEP RFIs

A dedicated TZV MEP BIM team operating as a seamless extension of a UK engineering practice — across five live projects simultaneously.

LocationUnited Kingdom (Remote)

The Challenge

What the client needed to solve

A mid-size mechanical and electrical engineering consultancy in the UK had secured five significant projects simultaneously — a combination of commercial fit-outs and residential developments — all requiring Revit MEP BIM deliverables. The firm had two Revit-competent engineers on staff, both committed to project management and design. They had neither the capacity nor the budget to hire additional in-house Revit staff on short notice, and the BIM requirements on all five projects were contractual. They needed a reliable MEP BIM team that could work to UK standards, use BIM 360, and deliver without constant supervision.

Our Solution

How The Zenith Visions delivered

The Zenith Visions assigned a dedicated three-person MEP BIM team to the engagement. After a two-day onboarding period covering the firm's Revit template, BIM 360 environment, ISO 19650 naming convention and each project's BIM Execution Plan, the team began modeling on all five projects in parallel. A weekly video coordination call with the firm's project leads replaced the need for daily oversight. Models were uploaded to BIM 360 every Friday for client review. Clash reports were generated and resolved before each fortnightly issue.

  • Dedicated 3-person MEP BIM team operating within the client's BIM 360 environment
  • ISO 19650-compliant naming and folder structure across all 5 projects
  • HVAC, plumbing, electrical and fire protection modeled to LOD 300–350
  • Weekly coordination calls replacing ad-hoc supervision overhead
  • Clash detection run fortnightly; all clashes resolved before contractor issue
  • All 5 projects delivered on contractual programme; no extensions requested
