Permit Set Preparation

Services / Permit Set Preparation

Permit-ready building drawings that satisfy plan check on the first submission.

A rejected permit set means delay, re-filing fees and a frustrated client. The Zenith Visions produces jurisdiction-specific permit drawing packages that are complete on first submission. We research your local building department's checklist, follow the applicable code (IBC, IRC, NCC or UK Building Regulations), and produce a package that reviewers can approve without additional information requests. Residential, commercial, tenant improvement and ADU permit sets are all within our scope.

Software

AutoCAD, Revit

Output

PDF, DWG, sheet set

Coverage

US, UK, Australia, Middle East

Lead time

3 to 10 business days

First-submission success

The permit reviewer's checklist, completed before you submit

We obtain or construct the plan check checklist for your jurisdiction before drawing a single line. Every required note, code reference, dimension, egress calculation and accessibility item is placed on the correct sheet before the package leaves our desk.

Code experience

Experienced across US, UK and Australian codes

Our team has produced permit sets to IBC 2021, IRC 2021, CBC, NCC (Australian National Construction Code) and UK Building Regulations Part A through Part M. When a jurisdiction requires a specific format, title block or accessibility schedule, we already know what that looks like.

Speed without shortcuts

Fast turnaround, nothing omitted

Permit set delays often come from submitting an incomplete package and waiting for a correction letter. Our pre-submission review catches omissions before they reach the reviewer. The result is fewer rounds, faster approval and a happier client.

What we deliver

Sub-services

Residential Permit Sets

New single-family homes, additions and alterations — site plan, floor plans, elevations, sections, structural notes, energy calculations and accessibility summary presented to your jurisdiction's format.

ADU & Garage Conversion Permit Drawings

Accessory Dwelling Unit permit packages including site coverage calculations, parking compliance, setback diagrams, floor plans and title 24 energy notes for California and other ADU-active jurisdictions.

Commercial Permit Sets

Mixed-use, retail, office and industrial permit packages with occupancy analysis, egress calculations, means of egress plans, fire-rated assembly details and accessibility compliance documentation.

Tenant Improvement Permit Drawings

Interior alteration permit sets for commercial tenants: demolition plans, new construction plans, mechanical, electrical and plumbing (MEP) coordination, and code-compliant exiting analysis.

Deferred Submittal Packages

Structural and MEP deferred submittal drawings for systems approved in concept but detailed post-permit: trusses, curtain wall, sprinkler and fire alarm systems.

Over-the-Counter Permit Drawings

Simple residential alterations and repairs formatted for over-the-counter or online permit applications — fast, clean and complete.

Common questions

FAQs

How do you know what a specific jurisdiction requires?

We research the plan check checklist and submittal requirements for every jurisdiction at the start of the project. For jurisdictions we have worked with before, we have an established format. For new ones, we obtain the requirements from the building department before starting.

Do you prepare the energy compliance documentation?

For US projects we include Title 24 compliance documentation (California) or IECC compliance notes as required by the jurisdiction. For other countries we follow the relevant energy standard.

Can you prepare permit sets for projects in other countries?

Yes. We have produced permit-equivalent packages for projects in Australia (NCC), the UAE (Dubai Municipality), and the UK (Building Regulations). We research the local submission format in each case.

What if the permit reviewer asks for corrections?

We handle plan check correction responses as part of the service. We review the correction letter, address each item and re-issue the affected sheets at no additional charge for comments arising from our drawing errors.
