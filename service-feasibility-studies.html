Feasibility Studies

Services / Feasibility Studies

The honest answer before you commit — site, code, schedule, and cost.

A feasibility study is the cheapest insurance policy you can buy. We tell you whether a project pencils out before you put real money on the table, with a written report you can take to your bank, your board, or your investors.

Engagement

Cost

Our take

Cheapest insurance you can buy

We do feasibility work on a fixed fee because the math should be transparent. You should know the price before you commit, and you should walk away with everything you need to make a real go/no-go decision.

How we work

Site, code, cost — then a clear answer

Site analysis covers zoning, height limits, easements, environmental, utilities. Code review covers building code, accessibility, fire. Cost modeling pulls from comparable projects and contractor input. The report is short and conclusive — not a brochure.
