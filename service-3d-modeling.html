3D Modeling

Services / 3D Modeling

Accurate 3D models, from product and component to full building, ready for any pipeline.

Welcome to The Zenith Visions, where innovation meets precision in 3D modeling. Our comprehensive range of 3D modeling services is tailored to architects, designers, engineers, and contractors who seek accuracy and efficiency.

Software

Revit, 3ds Max, SketchUp

Output

RVT, FBX, OBJ, SKP

Typical lead

2 to 10 days

Detail

Mesh and solid

Our take

Clean geometry travels well

A model that is messy under the hood causes problems downstream. We build tidy, well-organised geometry that imports cleanly into whatever tool comes next.

How we work

Modelled for its purpose

We model to suit the end use, lightweight for real-time, detailed for fabrication, parametric for BIM, so you are never paying for detail you cannot use.
