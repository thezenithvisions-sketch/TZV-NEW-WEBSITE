Lighting Design

Services / Lighting Design

Architectural lighting that supports the design and stays out of the way.

We treat light as a material — designed, specified, and modeled like any other — not as a finishing trim picked from a catalogue at the end. A good lighting design disappears; you notice the architecture, not the fixtures.

Our take

The cheapest way to ruin a great room

Lighting is often the cheapest way to ruin a great room. Our job is to keep that from happening — and to use light to do the heavy lifting that materials and finishes can’t.

How we work

Daylight first, then layers, then controls

We start with daylight analysis, then layer in architectural fixtures (downlights, linears, accents) chosen for color quality and cut-off. Controls are designed alongside the layout — scenes, dimming curves, occupancy logic. Mockups before commitment.
