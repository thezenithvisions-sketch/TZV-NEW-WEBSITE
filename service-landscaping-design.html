Landscaping Design

Services / Landscaping Design

Site design, planting, hardscape, and water systems that age into their setting.

Our landscapes serve the architecture and the climate in equal measure — designed to look better in year ten than they did at substantial completion. We work with native palettes wherever possible and design site water as a system, not an afterthought.

Engagement

4–12 months

Typical lead

3–6 weeks

Plant install

1–3 weeks per acre

Maintenance

Year-1 included

Our take

A 20-year design problem

A landscape is a 20-year design problem. The first three years are about establishment; the next seventeen are about how the design ages. We design for both.

How we work

Site, then water, then planting

Site analysis informs grading and water. Planting is selected for the long arc — native and adapted species, drought tolerance, succession planning. Hardscape is designed alongside, not bolted on.

Deliverables & capabilities

  • Native planting plans
  • Hardscape & paving
  • Sustainable irrigation
