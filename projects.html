Our Projects Portfolio

From early concept sketches to delivering outstanding completed buildings, we’re a small, focused crew ready to turn your project dreams into reality, with rigor, restraint, and a quiet love for the craft.

Hillside Oasis Residence
Private Residence Architecture & Interior

A 9,800 sq ft family home that disappears into the cliffside. Local stone, board-formed concrete, and a cantilevered yoga pavilion turn an exposed Malibu hilltop into a refuge, a study in restraint and the discipline of letting the landscape lead.

Year 2024
Location Malibu, California, USA
Serenity Suites Coastal Resort
Hospitality Architecture

An eight-villa coastal resort tucked into a Pacific bluff, designed for guests who want the ocean close but the world far away. Cross-ventilation, deep eaves, and outdoor showers do the work that air conditioning would have done elsewhere.

Year 2023
Location Pacific Shores, California, USA
Gastronome Haven
Hospitality Architecture & Interior

A 120-cover restaurant carved from a 1920s warehouse shell. We preserved the original timber trusses and steel rivets, then layered in warm brass, oak, and a sculptural open kitchen as the room’s centerpiece.

Year 2023
Location Downtown Metropolis
Serene Haven Residences
Residential Architecture

Nestled amid the picturesque vineyards of Napa Valley, Serene Haven was envisioned as a sanctuary that seamlessly blended with its natural setting. Our challenge was to craft a residence that not only provided a luxurious living experience but also minimized its ecological footprint through sustainable design solutions.

Year 2022
Location Napa Valley, California, USA
Art Museum Expansion
Commercial Cultural

A 40,000 sq ft contemporary wing added to a beaux-arts museum without competing with it. A cantilevered glass volume floats above the historic building, the connection knitted by a quiet steel-and-glass bridge.

Year 2023
Location Chicago, Illinois, USA
CloudSmart Corporate HQ
Commercial LEED Platinum

A LEED Platinum headquarters built around natural ventilation, indoor planting, and a workplace ethos that puts focus before flash. CloudSmart wanted a building their team actually wanted to come into, and that’s what we built.

Year 2023
Location Austin, Texas, USA
TerraBotanics Grand Botanical
Commercial Landscape

A visitor pavilion and conservatory for a public botanical garden. Mass-timber arches form a canopy, and a rainwater system feeds the gardens it sits within, architecture as a quiet servant of the landscape.

Year 2022
Location Portland, Oregon, USA
Heritage Haven Restoration
Historic Restoration

A meticulous restoration of an 1890s townhouse, returning it to its original Italianate plan while quietly integrating modern systems. Period plasterwork, reclaimed millwork, and an entirely new mechanical core, invisible by design.

Year 2022
Location Boston, Massachusetts, USA
Twilight Loft Apartments
Residential Adaptive Reuse

Sixteen loft apartments inside a former bookbindery. The bones, cast-iron columns and a sawtooth roof, were the brief. We let the building’s history set the tone and quietly added everything a contemporary home needs.

Year 2023
Location Brooklyn, New York, USA
The Ember Pavilion
Hospitality Pavilion

A four-season hospitality pavilion at the foot of the Aspen ski runs. Heavy timber and rusted-steel cladding work with the alpine context; a wood-burning hearth anchors the central lounge as the literal and figurative heart.

Year 2024
Location Aspen, Colorado, USA
Skyloft Office Tower
Commercial Mixed-use

A 28-story office tower with a 7-story timber crown and a public market at grade. The mass-timber upper floors carry less embodied carbon than a comparable steel design, and the ground floor returns space to the city.

Year 2024
Location Seattle, Washington, USA
Half Moon Bay Library
Historic Civic

A civic library rebuilt around a single mature oak. The reading room curves to meet the tree’s canopy, daylight is borrowed instead of imported, and the program orbits a community room that the town actually uses.

Year 2021
Location Half Moon Bay, California, USA
