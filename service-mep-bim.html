MEP BIM Service

Services / MEP BIM Service

Fully coordinated mechanical, electrical and plumbing models, clash-free before you break ground.

At The Zenith Visions, we excel in providing state-of-the-art MEP BIM Services, revolutionizing how mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems integrate with technology. Our expertise lies in creating intelligent, data-enriched 3D models that optimize design coordination.

Software

Revit MEP, Navisworks

Level of detail

LOD 200 to 400

Typical lead

5 to 14 days

Output

RVT, NWC, DWG

Our take

Clash-free is the baseline

Reworking services on site is one of the most expensive mistakes a project can make. We resolve the clashes in the model, where a fix costs minutes instead of days.

How we work

Every discipline in one space

Mechanical, electrical and plumbing are modelled against the architectural and structural backgrounds and tested together. You get a single coordinated model and a clash report you can actually sign off on.

What we offer

Sub-services

Mechanical BIM Modeling

HVAC, ductwork and equipment modelled and coordinated in Revit.

Electrical BIM Modeling

Power, lighting and containment routed and modelled for clean coordination.

Plumbing BIM Modeling

Water, drainage and fire protection systems modelled to spec.

MEP Documentation

Coordination drawings, schedules and spool sheets ready for site.
