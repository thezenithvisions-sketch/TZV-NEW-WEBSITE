Construction Management

Services / Construction Management

Owner’s-rep services from groundbreaking through final inspection.

We protect your design intent, your schedule, and your budget on the job site — coordinating contractors, consultants, and inspectors so you don’t have to. CM is the most expensive service to skip on a project that’s gone sideways.

RFIs

Our take

Where drawings become buildings

Construction administration is where great drawings either become great buildings or get diluted. We staff CM with senior architects, not interns. The fee is real money and worth every dollar.

How we work

Pre-con, build, closeout

Pre-construction we review bids, qualify contractors, and build a realistic schedule. During construction we visit weekly, respond to RFIs within 48 hours, and write change orders before they become disputes. At closeout we walk the building with you and the owner’s facility team.

Deliverables & capabilities

  • Schedule & cost oversight
  • Quality control on site
  • RFI & submittal review
  • Change order management
  • Substantial completion & closeout
