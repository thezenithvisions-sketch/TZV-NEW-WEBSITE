Architecture BIM Service

Services / Architecture BIM Service

Construction-ready architectural models that carry your design intent from concept to site.

At The Zenith Visions, we specialize in delivering cutting-edge Architectural BIM Services that redefine the way architecture meets technology. Our approach focuses on creating intelligent, data-rich 3D models that streamline the design, documentation, and execution of architectural projects, ensuring unmatched precision and efficiency can bring your vision to life.

Software

Revit, ArchiCAD

Level of detail

LOD 100 to 500

Typical lead

3 to 10 days

Output

RVT, IFC, DWG, PDF

Our take

An extension of your studio

Think of us as in-house capacity you can scale up or down. We plug into your standards, your template and your naming conventions, so what comes back feels like your own team produced it, only faster.

How we work

Coordinated from day one

Every model is built to a clear LOD brief and checked against the other disciplines before it reaches you. Fewer surprises on site, fewer RFIs, and a single source of truth everyone can trust.

What we offer

Sub-services

Architecture BIM Modeling

Sheets, schedules and construction documentation generated straight from the model.

Site, terrain and landscape elements modelled and coordinated with the building.
