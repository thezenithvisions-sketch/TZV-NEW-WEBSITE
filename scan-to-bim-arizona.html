FAQs

Scan to BIM in Arizona — common questions

Do you provide Scan to BIM services across Arizona?

Yes. We work remotely with surveyors, architects, engineers and contractors throughout Arizona, including Phoenix, Tucson, Mesa, Scottsdale. You send the registered or raw point cloud (E57, RCP, LAS or LAZ) and we deliver accurate as-built Revit models, with no need for us to be on site.

What level of detail can you deliver for Arizona projects?

Anything from LOD 100 massing to LOD 500 verified as-built. We model architecture, structure and MEP to the LOD your project needs, and run a deviation check against the point cloud so you can see exactly how closely the model tracks the real building.

How do you handle Arizona standards and BIM requirements?

We deliver to the US National CAD Standard and owner BIM requirements, using your Revit template, title block and naming convention so the model drops straight into your workflow. If you do not have a standard set up, we can advise on one.

How accurate and how fast is your Scan to BIM service?

We typically hold modelled geometry within a few millimetres of the point cloud, and most data centers and commercial jobs are delivered in 5 to 15 working days depending on size and LOD — usually at 40–60% less than equivalent in-house production.
