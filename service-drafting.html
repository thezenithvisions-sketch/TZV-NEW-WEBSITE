Drafting Services

Services / Drafting Services

Precise 2D drafting and documentation, fast turnaround, every line where it should be.

At The Zenith Visions, we pride ourselves on delivering precise and high-quality drafting services tailored to meet the needs of architects, engineers, contractors, and designers. Whether you need detailed CAD drafting, permit set documentation, or interior design documentation.

Software

AutoCAD, Revit

Output

DWG, PDF

Typical lead

1 to 5 days

Formats

2D and full sheet sets

Our take

Speed without sloppiness

Drafting is where deadlines are won or lost. We move fast, but every sheet is checked against your standards so you are never cleaning up after us.

How we work

Your standards, our hands

Send us your template, layer convention and a markup. We work inside your system so the drawings drop straight into your set with no reformatting.

What we offer

Sub-services

CAD Documentation Service

Clean, standards-compliant construction and permit sets in AutoCAD.

CAD Detailing Service

Precise details and sections drawn to your title block and layer standards.

PDF to CAD Conversion

Accurate, fully editable CAD files converted from PDFs or scans.

Permit Sets & ADU Drawings

Submission-ready permit and ADU drawing sets for your jurisdiction.
